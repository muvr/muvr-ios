Swissball workout

We bet you have seen these, you've probably even sat on one ... maybe fallen off?!

These balls are great pieces of equipment and should be tremendous amount of fun. They are designed to increase your balance, coordination whilst intensely targeting the deep core muscles.

Instructions

For the swissball exercises, begin with 2 sets of 15 and have 30 seconds rest between exercises.

For the plank position perform 2 sets of 30 seconds.

For the exercises with dumbbells, ensure the weight is heavy enough to only just complete the rep range.
