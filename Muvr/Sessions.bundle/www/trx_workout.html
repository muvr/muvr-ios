The TRX workout

Do not be scared of this piece of equipment, be very excited! If you are looking for something different, follow this program for the next 4 weeks. The TRX is a suspension training system, which will fire up muscles you did not know existed.

This system uses your natural body weight as a resistance, meaning it is quick and easy to increase or decrease the workload.

Instructions

Complete the number of reps associated with the exercise, rest for 30 seconds and complete a second set.

If you have any questions regarding the setup of the TRX it is important you see a member of the personal training team.

Ensure you complete a thorough warmup before you start this workout, and a thorough warm down and full body stretch afterwards.
