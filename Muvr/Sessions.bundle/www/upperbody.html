Upperbody workout

Do you have a night out planned? It could be a birthday celebration or simply a regular Saturday night, but we bet you want to look great and fill your t-shirt in all the right places! On the day of your night out, do this workout to look and feel great.

Instructions

Complete 2 sets of 15 repetitions with no more than 30 seconds rest between sets. The weight you lift should be heavy enough fo you just to complete the rep range with perfect form.

Ensure you complete a thorough warmup before you start this workout, and a thorough warmdown and full body stretch afterwards.
