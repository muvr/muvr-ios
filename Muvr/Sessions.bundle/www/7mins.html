The 7 minutes workout

The 7 minutes workout is based on high intensity circuit training and has been proved the most "safe, effective and efficient" way to improve your muscles and health.

It only takes about 7 minutes, which consists of 13 exercises. Each exercise is to be taken 30 seconds with 10 seconds intervals between exercises. All you need is a chair and a wall.

Repeat 2-3 circuits according to your time.

The 7 minutes workout is for the time-constrained people to improve physical energy in very short time. It can be done anywhere

The program trains the whole body muscle with extremely intense activities by using the resistance of the body weight. And it really works: "maximum results with minimal investment".

The benefits

The 7 minutes workout is a fast and efficient way to lose weight and fat because the resistance training contributes significantly to the fat burned during a workout.

High intensity circuit training (HICT) may be an extremely effective and efficient means to increase an individual's V.BO2max, which can improve cardiopulmonary health.

Exercise order

You should follow the 7 minutes HICT order, which can train muscle groups during the consecutive exercises by alternating rest and exercise.

Individual exercise time

30 seconds is sufficient for each exercise to be taken 12-15 times.

Total exercise time

It is suggested that you repeat 2-3 circuits, that is at least 20 minutes per session
