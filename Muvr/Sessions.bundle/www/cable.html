The cable workout

The 8-stack multi station and functional trainer are fantastic pieces of kit that can act as a one stop shop for an entire body workout!

They combine cables with different attachments to provide an endless list of exercises!

This program will help you develop a strong, toned core. You may only think you are working your arms, but it is the body positioning that really makes your abs work too.

Instructions

Begin with 2 sets of 12 and have a 30 seconds rest between exercises. If this is easy, then increase the weight and shorten the rest period to 15 seconds.

Ensure you complete a thorough warmup before you start this workout, and a thorough warm down and full body stretch afterwards.
