Abs workout

Do you want a flat tummy? Even a rippling six pack? If so, you need to be doing lots of toning exercises to whip your abs into shape - and this program is a great place to begin!

Instructions

Perform each exercise for 30 seconds with 30 seconds rest between exercises. When this becomes easier, repeat the sequence once more and reduce the rest perdiods down to 15 seconds.

It is recommended that you begin your workouts with this core routine so you are fresh and full of energy, alternatively perform at the end of your workout to make it more challenging.

Ensure you completea thorough warmup before you start this workout, and a thorough warmdown and full body stretch afterwards.
