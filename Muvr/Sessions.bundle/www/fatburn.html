Fatburn workout

This program will burn maximum calories when you're working out and supercharge your metabolism for when you're not ... the end is tremendous fat loss! Providing your diet is well balanced!

Instructions

Perform this workout as a circuit moving from one exercise to the next, using the transition time as a rest period. Begin by completing it twice in one workout and build up to 3 times

For the strength exercises work continuously for 60 seconds and then move to the next exercise
