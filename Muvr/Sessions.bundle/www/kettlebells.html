Kettlebells workout

Kettlebells add great variation to your workout! Integrate this to your regular routine one per week to boost your results!

Instructions

Women begin with a 6kg kettlebell and quickly progress to an 8kg kettlebell.

Men begin with a 12kg kettlebell and quickly progress to a 16kg kettlebell.

Perform this workout as a mini circuit with no more than 30 seconds rest between exercises. At the end of the circuit have a 60 seconds rest and repeat.

Ensure you complpetea thorough warmuop before you start this workout, and a thorough warm down and full body stretch afterwards.
