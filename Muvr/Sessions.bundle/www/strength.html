Strength workout

This program will take you back to basics, using good compound movements to build strength.

These exercises will give you a total body workout. They are big muscle building moves, which will also supercharge your metabolism, meaning you will definitely be hungry after it!

Instructions

Complete 3 to 4 sets of 6 repetitions with no more than 60 seconds rest between sets. With the weight you select you should only just be able to complete the 6 reps.

Ensure you complete a thorough warmup before you start this workout, and a thorough warm down and full body stretch afterwards.
